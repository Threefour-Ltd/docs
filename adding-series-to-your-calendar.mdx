<Info>
  Calendar sync is available with a **Racemob Premium** plan
</Info>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Apple Calendar, Proton Calendar, Outlook etc.">
    Go to `Calendar` and click the `Add to calendar` button. You'll then be able to add the subscription URL, customise the name and set any reminder preferences.

    Your calendar will update automatically when sessions are added or updated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Google Calendar">
    Go to `Calendar` and click the `Copy subscription link` button.

    You will need to enter this URL on the [web version of Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com). Scroll down to `Other calendars` and choose `Import from URL`. Use the link you copied earlier and click `Add calendar`.

    Your calendar will update automatically when sessions are added or updated.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
