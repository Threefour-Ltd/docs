---
title: "Reset calendar link"
sidebarTitle: "Reset calendar link"
icon: "link"
---

If you need to reset your calendar link because it's been compromised or you accidentally shared it with someone, go to `Calendar` \> `Security` \> `Reset calendar link`.

<Warning>
  **This will invalidate your current link.** You'll need to update your subscription link in your calendar app.
</Warning>
